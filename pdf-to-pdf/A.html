Convert PDF to PDF/A

Convert your PDF files to PDF/A format for long-term archiving. PDF/A is an ISO-standardized version of PDF specialized for digital preservation.

Why Use PDF/A:

  • Ensures document can be reproduced exactly in the future
  • Embedded fonts and self-contained
  • No encryption or external references
  • ISO standard for document archiving
