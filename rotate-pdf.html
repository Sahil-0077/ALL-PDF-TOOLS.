How to Rotate PDF Pages

  1. Upload your PDF file with pages that need rotating.
  2. Select the rotation direction (clockwise, counter-clockwise, or 180° flip).
  3. Specify which pages to rotate (leave blank for all pages).
  4. Click the "Rotate PDF" button to apply the changes.
  5. Download your rotated PDF document.
