How to Protect a PDF

  1. Upload your PDF file that needs protection.
  2. Set passwords for opening and/or restricting permissions.
  3. Choose which permissions to allow (printing, editing, etc.).
  4. Click the "Protect PDF" button to secure your document.
  5. Download your password-protected PDF.
