How to Split a PDF

  1. Upload your PDF file by dropping it in the designated area or clicking the "Select PDF File" button.
  2. Choose your preferred splitting method:
    • Split by all pages: Creates a separate PDF for each page.
    • Split by page ranges: Divides your PDF according to the ranges you specify.
    • Split by custom page numbers: Splits the PDF at specific page numbers.
  3. Click the "Split PDF" button to process your file.
  4. Download your split PDF files individually or as a ZIP archive.
